"11th Conference of the Polish Society on Relativity" 2025

Date: 21-24/07/2025

Type: Conference

"XLII Workshop on Geometrical Methods in Physics" 2025

Date: 30/06-05/07/2025
Location: University of Białystok, Białystok, Poland
Type: Conference
The workshop featured a wide range of topics in mathematics and some in mathematical physics, with emphasis on noncommutative geometry. Discussions frequently addressed algebraic structures and deformations of spacetime symmetries. My poster on κ-Deformed Special Relativity aligned closely with these themes and generated meaningful engagement. The event fostered insightful exchanges and offered a valuable platform for presenting current research in theoretical physics.

Photo courtesy of the event organiser.

“XXIII Interdisciplinary Math-Science Student Conference” SeMPowisko 2025

Date: 22-25/05/2025
Location: Jagiellonian University, Kraków, Poland
Type: Student Conference
Michał Popiel and I presented a poster together on “Time delays in κ-Deformed Special Relativity”. We provided a comparison between the classical and quantum limits of this hypothesis and detailed how time delays can arise from both aspects.

"Searching for Quantum Gravity in the Sky" 2025

Date: 16-21/02/2025
Location: Bad Honnef, Germany
Type: Student School
I attended a physics school focused on quantum gravity phenomenology, which brought together master’s and Ph.D. students from physics, mathematics, and philosophy. The program offered a comprehensive introduction to both theoretical and experimental aspects of the field, with lectures on topics such as deformed relativity, time delays in astrophysical observations, and quantum gravity effects on particle propagation.
During the school, I presented a scientific poster related to noncommutative hypotheses, specifically within the framework of κ-Deformed Special Relativity. We also had the opportunity to visit Europe’s largest movable-dish telescope, which provided valuable insight into observational techniques relevant to quantum gravity research.
