2025/26 - Winter semester - Analiza matematyczna 3

Tutorial class for Professor Trześniewski’s lecture. Conducted in Polish. The class takes place in room 282 on Tuesdays from 11:15 to 13:45.

The course is focused on the theory of differential equations, Fourier series and distributions. It aims at practical skills of solving a Cauchy problem in abstract and physical realisations.
