Fizyka z Kafką (Physics with Kafka)

Date: ongoing, my talk: 02/02/2026
Location: Księgarnia Hiszpańska Wrocław La Librería Española de Wrocław
Type: open talks
The café “Księgarnia Hiszpańska Wrocław – La Librería Española de Wrocław” hosts a series of meetings entitled “Physics with Kafka”. These events are organised by Dr Aneta Wojnar and the Physics Students’ Society “Migacz”, and aim to bring fascinating and up-to-date topics in physics closer to a wider audience — to show people that physics can be (and above all is!) interesting, and that it touches all of our lives.
As part of this series, I will have the pleasure of giving a talk in Polish entitled “Czarne dziury w laboratorium - czyli o analogach grawitacji” (“Black Holes in the Laboratory – Analogues of Gravity”), in which I will discuss the most common ways in which General Relativity is imagined, the pitfalls such comparisons can lead to, and experiments that test the mathematics of GR under entirely different conditions.
